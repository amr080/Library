Additional Endpoints - Yield Management
GET /v1/usdx/yield/rate - Current yield rate
GET /v1/usdx/yield/accrued/{walletId} - Accrued yield for wallet
POST /v1/usdx/yield/distribute - Distribute yield
GET /v1/usdx/nav - Net Asset Value calculation

Token Operations
POST /v1/usdx/wrap - Wrap USDX to wUSDX
POST /v1/usdx/unwrap - Unwrap wUSDX to USDX

YieldAccrual
