Core Functions
POST /onboard/kyc - KYC/compliance
POST /mint/usdc - USDC to USDX ($5k min)
POST /redeem/usdc - USDX to USDC ($5k min)
GET /balance/total - Total holdings

Yield Management
GET /yield/current - Show daily rate
GET /yield/accrued - View earned yield
POST /yield/reinvest - Auto-compound
GET /yield/nav - Get current NAV

USDX <> wUSDX
POST /wrap - Convert to wUSDX
POST /unwrap - Convert to USDX
GET /shares - Get share balance

Reporting
GET /reporting/daily - Daily statements
GET /reporting/monthly - Monthly reports
GET /reporting/audit - Audit verification
