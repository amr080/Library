RegTech

XFT's blockchain is designed to meet the varying rates of adoption and regulatory frameworks to ensure compliant asset trading worldwide. With XFT's compliance dApp and oracle, regulators can customize, maintain, update, and realize regulatory compliance.

  • 1. Token Wrapping

  • 2. Geoledgers

  • 3. Regulatory Oracles

How it works

  • Assets are tokenized and wrapped with regulatory compliance hooks.
  • Tokens are issued on appropriate geoledgers based on jurisdiction.
  • Regulatory oracles continuously monitor and update compliance requirements.
  • Transactions are checked against regulatory oracles for compliance.
  • Compliant transactions are executed and recorded on relevant geoledgers.
