GET /v1/businessAccount/balances: Retrieve available business account balances.

POST /v1/businessAccount/payouts: Create a new payout.

GET /v1/businessAccount/payouts: List all payouts.

GET /v1/businessAccount/payouts/{id}: Get details of a specific payout.

GET /v1/businessAccount/banks/wires: List wire bank accounts.

GET /v1/businessAccount/banks/wires/{id}: Get details of a specific wire bank account.

GET /v1/businessAccount/banks/wires/{id}/instructions: Get wire transfer instructions.

POST /v1/mocks/payments/wire: Create a mock wire payment (sandbox).

GET /v1/businessAccount/banks/cubix/{id}/instructions: Get CUBIX transfer instructions.
